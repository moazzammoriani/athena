Who are we?

We are a group of individuals brought together by our passion to learn about the world. In order to improve our experience of learning and to make the most of our limited time here, we want to learn as efficiently as possible. One of the best ways to learn is through engagement with others, and this is why we formed Athena - so that people interested in learning can help each other out and share their expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Athena Collective

Do I have to pay to become a part of the community?

Not at all! You only have to pay if you wish to become a pro user. You can gain most of the benefits of the community just by being a basic user.

If I can get most benefits with basic membership, why choose pro?

The pro membership gives you access to our online library, including scholarly journals and ebook databases. These premium resources provide significant value for serious learners.

How can I work with you?

Our community thrives thanks to amazing volunteers who contribute their efforts. You're welcome to join us! Please send us an email to get started.
