About Us

Who are we?

We are a group of individuals who are brought together by our passion to learn about the world. In order to improve our experience of learning and to make the most of our limited time here we want to learn as efficiently as possible. One of the best ways to learn is to learn from others through engagement ans this is why we formed Athena. So that other people who are also interested in learning anything about the world can help each other out and share whatever there expertise is in.

What are our goals?

We wish to bring together an online community to promote self-learning. We wish to enrich the lives of everyone who seeks to know more about the world by allowing them to develop the cognitive toolkit necessary to reach such a goal more easily.

FAQ

Do I have to pay to become a part of the community?

Not at all! You only have to pay if you wish to become a pro user. You can gain most of the benefits of the community just by being a basic user.

If I can get most of the benefits by having a basic memebership then why is there a pro membership option even available?

The purpose of the pro membership is to give people access to the online library. The pro membership gives members access to scholarly journals and and ebook databases. So we believe those options have value worth paying for.

How can I work for you?

Our community is made possible thanks to the many volunteers who contribute their efforts into it. You are welcome to volunteer. Kindly send us an email.
